Notice of Special Meeting 
 
The Board will hold a hearing on Wednesday, August 26, 2015 at 10:00 a.m. The 
board will consider the filing of an appeal of the board of elections decision to not certify 
Mr. O. Danny Mapp to the ballot as a candidate for member of the board of education of 
the Springfield City School District. The board will discuss and/or vote upon any 
business or matter that may lawfully come before the Board. 
 
The Board will conduct the meeting at the office of the Board of Elections located at 
3130 E. Main St., Springfield, OH 45505.
