The post-election audit meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order 
by Chairman Thomas Ferrell on Wednesday, December 4th, 2024 at 3:00 p.m.   
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman                         Present  
(R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member   Present 
                        (D) MaryAnn Groot, Member               Present 
(R) John C. Adams, Member   Present 
 
 
Guests in attendance: Amy Grubbe 
 
Adrienne James, Deputy Director, stated that we conducted the post-election audit on 
Monday at 10:00 a.m.  The four polling locations we audited were Huron First 
Presbyterian Church, Sandusky Firehouse, Vermilion Veterans Field House, and Berlin 
Heights Municipal Building.  We audited a total of 2,237 ballots.  The three races we 
audited were President, U.S. Senator, and State Issue 1.  The numbers from our hand 
count match 100% with the numbers from our official certification.  The board members, 
director, and deputy director signed the post-election audit workbook to submit to the 
Secretary of Stateâ€™s office.   
 
Ms. Crescimano motioned to certify the results of the post-election audit, with Ms. Groot 
seconding.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.   
 
Ms. Groot stated she was not able to make the regular monthly meeting on December 19th.  
The board members decided to reschedule it for Wednesday, December 18th at 9:00 a.m.  
 
Ms. Crescimano motioned to adjourn, with Ms. Groot seconding.  The board adjourned at 
3:05 p.m.
