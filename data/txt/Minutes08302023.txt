The special meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order by 
Chairman Thomas Ferrell on Wednesday, August 30th, 2023 at 9:00 a.m.   
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman                         Present  
(D) William J. Monaghan, Member  Present 
                        (R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member               Present 
                        (R) Nancy C. McKeen, Member              Present 
 
Guests in attendance: MaryAnn Groot and Amy Grubbe 
 
Mr. Ferrell stated the purpose of the meeting is to certify write-in candidates for the 
November 7th General Election.  Alex Jones, Director, stated there are two write-in 
candidates for the board to review for Huron City Board of Education.  Ms. Crescimano 
made a motion to certify the write-in candidates, with Mr. Monaghan seconding.  All board 
members voted yes; motion carried.  The candidates are John Adams and Chris Rager.  
 
Mr. Jones stated that Connie Ward filed to be a write-in candidate for Berlin Heights 
mayor.  She also filed a petition to run for Berlin Heights mayor that was rejected by the 
board.  The ORC and EOM state that if the board rejects a nominating petition, the person 
cannot file as a write-in.  Ms. Crescimano made a motion to reject Ms. Ward’s write-in 
candidacy, with Mr. Monaghan seconding.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.  
 
Ms. Grubbe asked if write-in candidates’ names are posted at the polling locations.  
Adrienne James, Deputy Director, stated no, we are not permitted to do so.  
 
Ms. Crescimano made a motion to adjourn, with Mr. Monaghan seconding.  All board 
members voted yes; motion carried.  Board adjourned at 9:04 a.m.
