The Board will complete the Official Canvass of the June 7, 2016 Special congressional 
General Election on Saturday, June 18, 2016 at 10:00 a.m. The Board will meet at the 
Board of Elections office located at 3130 E. Main St., Springfield, OH 45505. 
 
The board will, at this time, determine the validity and sufficiency of the 
provisional ballots cast on and before June 7, 2016.  
 
The board will discuss and/or vote upon any business or matter that may lawfully come 
before the Board.
