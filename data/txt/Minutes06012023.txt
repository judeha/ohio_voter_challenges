The post-election audit meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order 
by Board Member William Monaghan on Thursday, June 1st, 2023 at 2:00 p.m.       
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman   Absent (came in later) 
(D) William J. Monaghan, Member  Present 
                        (R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member               Present 
                        (R) Nancy C. McKeen, Member              Present 
 
Guests in attendance: Amy Grubbe 
 
Mr. Monaghan made a motion to go into recess to conduct the post-election audit.  Ms. 
Crescimano seconded the motion.  All board members present voted yes; motion carried.  
Board recessed at 2:00 p.m.  
 
Mr. Monaghan made a motion to go back into regular session.  Ms. Crescimano seconded 
the motion.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.  Board resumed regular session 
at 2:19 p.m.  Mr. Ferrell was now present.  
 
Adrienne James, Deputy Director, stated that the results of our hand count matched 
perfectly with the results from the election software.  The machines we audited were the 
Huron Township #3 election day scanner and the Perkins Township #10 election day 
scanner.  The board members, director, and deputy signed the audit certification form to 
send to the Secretary of Stateâ€™s office.  
 
Mr. Monaghan made a motion to adjourn, with Ms. Crescimano seconding.  All board 
members voted yes; motion carried.  Board adjourned at 2:21 p.m.
