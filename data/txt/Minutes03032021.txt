The reorganizational meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order by 
Chairman Ferrell at 9:00 a.m. on Wednesday, March 3rd, 2021.  
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman                         Present 
(D) William J. Monaghan, Member  Present 
                        (R) Nicholas J. Smith, Member                          Present 
                        (R) Jeffrey N. Krabill, Member              Present 
 
Guests in attendance: Shomore Deniro and Amy Grubbe 
 
Motion to select Tom Ferrell as temporary chairperson was made by Mr. Monaghan, 
seconded by Mr. Smith.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.   
 
Motion to appoint Lori Salyers as Director was made by Mr. Smith, seconded by Mr. 
Krabill.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.  
 
Motion to appoint Adrienne James as Deputy Director was made by Mr. Monaghan, 
seconded by Mr. Ferrell.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.  
 
Motion to appoint Tom Ferrell as chairperson was made by Mr. Smith, seconded by Mr. 
Monaghan.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.  
 
Mr. Ferrell administered the oath of office to Ms. Salyers, Ms. James, and the two clerks, 
Eliza Link and Libby Grant.  Ms. James passed out the ethics form for all board employees 
and board members to sign.  Ms. James also passed out Form 308 to be filled out by 
herself, Mr. Ferrell, and Ms. Salyers.  Before adjourning, Mr. Monaghan iterated the need 
for the office staff to be cross trained on all office procedures.  Ms. Salyers stated they 
would work on that.   
 
Motion to adjourn was made by Mr. Smith, seconded by Mr. Krabill.  All board members 
voted yes; motion carried.  Board adjourned at 9:12 a.m.  
 
 
 
 
 
 
Lori J. Salyers, Director
