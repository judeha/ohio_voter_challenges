March 30, 2016 
 
The Board will begin the Official Canvass of the March 15, 2016 Primary Election on 
Friday, April 1, 2016 at 10:00 a.m. The Board will meet at the Board of Elections office 
located at 3130 E. Main St., Springfield, OH 45505. 
 
The board will, at this time, determine the validity and sufficiency of the 
provisional ballots cast on and before March 15, 2016.  
 
The board will discuss and/or vote upon any business or matter that may lawfully come 
before the Board.
