The special meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order by 
Chairman Thomas Ferrell on Wednesday, December 6th, 2023 at 9:00 a.m.   
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman                         Present  
(R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member   Present 
                        (D) MaryAnn Groot, Member               Present 
                        (R) Nancy C. McKeen, Member              Present 
 
Guests in attendance: Rachel Ruffer from the Ohio Secretary of State’s office 
 
The purpose of the special meeting was to consider the approval of bills and the return of 
grant monies.  Alex Jones, Director, stated we have an invoice from Johnson Controls to 
pay which will require a Then and Now Certificate.  It will be paid out of a fund with grant 
money from 2022.  Ms. Crescimano motioned to pay the bill, with Ms. Groot seconding.  
All board members voted yes; motion carried.   
 
Mr. Jones stated we need to return HAVA grant money to the state before the end of the 
year.  Of the $10,000 that we received, we’re returning a little more than $1,100 and $200 
in accumulated interest, for a total of $1,346.90.  Ms. Crescimano motioned to return the 
grant money, with Ms. McKeen seconding.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.   
 
Ms. Crescimano motioned to adjourn, with Ms. Groot seconding.  All board members 
voted yes; motion carried.  Board adjourned at 9:07 a.m.  
 
 
Alexander W. Jones, Director
