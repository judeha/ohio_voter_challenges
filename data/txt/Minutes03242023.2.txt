The post-election audit meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order 
by Chairman Thomas Ferrell on Friday, March 24th at 9:30 a.m.      
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman   Present  
(D) William J. Monaghan, Member  Present 
                        (R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member               Present 
                        (R) Nancy C. McKeen, Member              Present 
 
Guests in attendance: Jason Hinners from Erie County Prosecutor’s Office, MaryAnn 
Groot, Amy Grubbe, Hallie Lippert, Steve Lippert, and Caleb Stidham 
 
Ms. Crescimano made a motion to go into recess while the bipartisan board staff conducts 
the post-election audit.  Mr. Monaghan seconded the motion.  All board members voted 
yes; motion carried.   
 
Mr. Monaghan made a motion to reconvene with Ms. Crescimano seconding.  All board 
members voted yes; motion carried.  Board reconvened at 9:58 a.m.  
 
Adrienne James, Deputy Director, reviewed the results of the post-election audit.  She 
stated that we audited two machines, the Sandusky H & I DS200 and the Sandusky J & N 
DS200.  We audited a total of 321 ballots out of 2,606 ballots cast, which is 12.31%.  The 
results of our hand count match perfectly with the results from our election software.  The 
board members, director, deputy director, and two clerks signed the post-election audit 
paperwork to submit to the Secretary of State’s office.   
 
Ms. Crescimano made a motion to adjourn, with Mr. Monaghan seconding.  All board 
members voted yes; motion carried.  Board adjourned at 10:00 a.m.
