The Board will conduct the Official Canvass of the May 5, 2015 Special Election on 
Monday, May 15, 2017 at 10:00 a.m. The Board will meet at the Board of Elections 
office located at 3130 E. Main St., Springfield, OH 45505. 
 
The board will, at this time, determine the validity and sufficiency of the 
provisional ballots cast on and before May 2, 2017. 
 
The board will discuss and/or vote upon any business or matter that may lawfully come 
before the Board.
