September 8, 2015 
 
The Board will hold its regular monthly meeting on Friday, September 11th at 10:00 
a.m. (rescheduled from Tuesday, September 08, 2015). 
 
The Board will certify the validity and sufficiency of nominating petitions for the City of 
New Carlisle member of council race. It will also appoint precinct election officials and 
vote on the acquisition and allocation of voting equipment and ballots for the November 
3, 2015 General Election. The board will discuss and/or vote upon any business or 
matter that may lawfully come before the Board. 
 
The Board will conduct the meeting at the office of the Board of Elections located at 
3130 E. Main St., Springfield, OH 45505.
