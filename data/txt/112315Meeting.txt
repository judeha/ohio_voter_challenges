November 18, 2015 
 
The Board will conduct the Official Canvass of the November 3, 2015 General Election 
on Monday, November 23, 2015 at 9:00 a.m. The Board will meet at the Board of 
Elections office located at 3130 E. Main St., Springfield, OH 45505. 
 
The board will, at this time, determine the validity and sufficiency of the 
provisional ballots cast on and before November 3, 2015.  
 
The board will discuss and/or vote upon any business or matter that may lawfully come 
before the Board.
