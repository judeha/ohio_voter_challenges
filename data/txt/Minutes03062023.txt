The reorganization meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order by 
Chairman Thomas Ferrell on Monday, March 6th, 2023 at 9 a.m.     
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman                         Present  
(D) William J. Monaghan, Member  Present 
                        (R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member               Present 
                        (R) Nancy C. McKeen, Member              Present 
 
Guests in attendance: Jason Hinners from Erie County Prosecutor’s Office, MaryAnn 
Groot, Amy Grubbe, Alex Jones, Debra Nickoloff, Bang Cung Nguyen, Crystal Nguyen, 
and Caleb Stidham 
 
Mr. Monaghan made a motion to select Mr. Ferrell as the temporary chairperson.  Ms. 
Crescimano seconded the motion.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.   
 
Mr. Monaghan made a motion to appoint Adrienne James as the Director.  Mr. Ferrell 
seconded the motion.  Ms. James took a roll call vote.   
 
Mr. Ferrell- yes 
Mr. Monaghan- yes 
Ms. Crescimano- no 
Ms. McKeen- no.  
 
Mr. Hinners stated, just as a point of clarification, you’re making nominations at this 
point.  So the nominations are made, then you make your vote.  
 
Ms. Crescimano made a motion to nominate Alex Jones as the Director.  Ms. McKeen 
seconded the motion.   
 
Mr. Hinners noted there are two names up for nomination.  If there are no other 
nominations from the board, then we take a vote.  Mr. Ferrell asked if there were any other 
nominations.  There were not.  Ms. James took a roll call vote.   
 
Mr. Ferrell- Adrienne 
Mr. Monaghan- Adrienne 
Ms. Crescimano- Alex 
Ms. McKeen- Alex.  
 
Ms. Crescimano asked if we have to vote five times.  Mr. Hinners stated yes.  Ms. 
Crescimano asked if we can discuss.  Mr. Hinners stated that is correct.  Then, if there are 
five dead-locked votes, it goes to the Secretary of State.   
 
Mr. Ferrell asked if we can go into executive session.  Mr. Hinners asked what would be the 
nature of the executive session.  Mr. Ferrell commented it’s the same way the county 
commissioners do it.  Mr. Hinners stated it could be for consideration of appointment of a 
public official.  He stated that technically the director is a public official, so you are 
permitted to go into executive session.   
 
Mr. Monaghan made a motion to go into executive session, with Ms. Crescimano 
seconding.  Ms. James took a roll call vote.   
 
Mr. Ferrell- yes 
Mr. Monaghan- yes 
Ms. Crescimano- yes 
Ms. McKeen- yes 
 
The board entered executive session at 9:05 a.m.  They reconvened at 9:20 a.m.

Ms. Crescimano made a motion to appoint Alex Jones as executive director.  Ms. McKeen 
seconded the motion.  Mr. Hinners stated, just to step back again, we’re on the second 
ballot, so we’re going to vote the same way.  We have two nominees on the table and we’ll 
do a roll call vote.  Mr. Monaghan moved to appoint Adrienne James as the Director.  Mr. 
Ferrell seconded the motion.  Mr. Hinners stated that in the roll call vote, you will say who 
your vote is for.  Ms. James took the roll.  
 
Mr. Ferrell- Adrienne 
Mr. Monaghan- Adrienne 
Ms. Crescimano- Alex 
Ms. McKeen- Alex.  
 
Ms. Crescimano moved to appoint Alex as executive director.  Ms. McKeen seconded the 
motion.  Mr. Monaghan motioned to appoint Adrienne as Director.  Mr. Ferrell seconded.  
Ms. James took the roll.  
 
Mr. Ferrell- Adrienne 
Mr. Monaghan- Adrienne 
Ms. Crescimano- Alex 
Ms. McKeen- Alex.  
 
Ms. Crescimano moved to appoint Alex as executive director.  Ms. McKeen seconded the 
motion.  Mr. Monaghan motioned to appoint Adrienne as Director.  Mr. Ferrell seconded.  
Ms. James took the roll.  
 
Mr. Ferrell- Adrienne 
Mr. Monaghan- Adrienne 
Ms. Crescimano- Alex 
Ms. McKeen- Alex.  
 
Ms. Crescimano moved to appoint Alex as executive director.  Ms. McKeen seconded the 
motion.  Mr. Monaghan motioned to appoint Adrienne as Director.  Mr. Ferrell seconded.  
Ms. James took the roll.  
 
Mr. Ferrell- Adrienne 
Mr. Monaghan- Adrienne 
Ms. Crescimano- Alex 
Ms. McKeen- Alex.  
 
Ms. Crescimano stated, “I move to recess until such time as the Secretary of State makes a 
decision on the executive director, and Adrienne retains duty as Deputy Director until such 
time as we reconvene in organizational meeting.”  Mr. Monaghan seconded the motion.  All 
board members voted yes; motion carried.  Board recessed at 9:25 a.m.  
 
 
Adrienne G. James, Deputy Director
