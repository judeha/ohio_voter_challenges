November 16, 2016 
 
The Board will begin the Official Canvass of the November 8, 2016 General Election on 
Monday, November 22, 2016 at 10:00 a.m. The Board will meet at the Board of 
Elections office located at 3130 E. Main St., Springfield, OH 45505. 
 
The board will, at this time, determine the validity and sufficiency of the 
provisional ballots cast on and before November 8, 2016.  
 
The board will discuss and/or vote upon any business or matter that may lawfully come 
before the Board.
