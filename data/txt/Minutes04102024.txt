The post-election audit meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order 
by Chairman Thomas Ferrell on Wednesday, April 10th, 2024 at 9:00 a.m.   
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman                         Present  
(R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member   Present 
                        (D) MaryAnn Groot, Member               Present 
 
Guests in attendance: Rachel Ruffer from Ohio Secretary of State’s Office 
 
Ms. Groot motioned to recess, with Ms. Crescimano seconding.  All board members voted 
yes; motion carried.  Board recessed at 9:00 a.m.  While in recess, bipartisan board staff 
conducted the post-election audit.  
 
At 10:00 a.m., the board reconvened with all members present.  
 
Adrienne James, Deputy Director, reviewed the results of the post-election audit.  Staff 
audited three races: Democratic State Supreme Court Justice, Republican Delegate-at-
Large, and the Erie MetroParks levy.  The three locations audited were Kelleys Island 
School (Kelleys Island Village), The Chapel (Perkins Township #10), and the VFW 
(Vermilion Townships #3, 4, and 5.)  The results from our hand count match 100% with 
the results from the voting machines.  The board members, director, and deputy director 
signed the certification of results page to submit to the Secretary of State’s Office.   
 
Ms. Crescimano motioned to adjourn, with Ms. Groot seconding.  All board members 
voted yes; motion carried.  Board adjourned at 10:02 a.m.
