The post-election audit meeting of the Erie County Board of Elections was called to order 
by Chairman Thomas Ferrell on Monday, August 28th, 2023 at 10:00 a.m.    
 
Roll Call:       (D) Thomas M. Ferrell, Chairman                         Present  
(D) William J. Monaghan, Member  Present 
                        (R) Lisa M. Crescimano, Member               Present 
                        (R) Nancy C. McKeen, Member              Present 
 
Guests in attendance: Rachel Ruffer from the SOS office and Amy Grubbe 
 
Ms. Crescimano made a motion to recess while bipartisan board staff conducts the post-
election audit.  Mr. Monaghan seconded the motion.  All board members voted yes; motion 
carried.  Board recessed at 10:01 a.m.  
 
At 10:36 a.m., the board returned to regular session.  Adrienne James, Deputy Director, 
stated that staff audited two election day voting machines: the scanner from the Sandusky 
Cultural Center, which hosts Sandusky H and I, and the scanner from Ritter Public 
Library, which hosts Vermilion 1-A, 2-A, and 2-B.  The results from our hand count match 
perfectly with the results from the scanners.   
 
The board members took a few moments to review the audit tally sheets and sign the audit 
certification page.  Mr. Monaghan made a motion to certify the results of the post-election 
audit, with Ms. Crescimano seconding.  All board members voted yes; motion carried.  
 
Ms. McKeen motioned to adjourn, with Mr. Monaghan seconding.  All board members 
voted yes; motion carried.  Board adjourned at 10:39 a.m.
